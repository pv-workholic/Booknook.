Hollywood legend Evelyn Hugo finally decides to reveal the truth behind her glamorous and controversial life. Through ambition, heartbreak, fame, sacrifice, and love, the novel slowly exposes the loneliness hidden beneath old Hollywood elegance.

The writing feels cinematic, emotional, dramatic, and deeply human all at once.
