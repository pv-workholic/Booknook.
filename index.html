The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Hollywood legend Evelyn Hugo finally reveals the truth behind her glamorous life, controversial marriages, fame, ambition, heartbreak, and the love she spent years protecting.

The novel feels cinematic, addictive, dramatic, and deeply emotional beneath all the glamour. It explores loneliness hidden inside fame, reinvention as survival, and the sacrifices people make for love.
